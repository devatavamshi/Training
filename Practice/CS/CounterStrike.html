New CS : GO Merchandise

There are some big additions to the CS:GO merchandise family, Series 2 Pins and the Hyper Beast Gaming Mouse and Mousepad.


PINS

At ESL One Cologne we introduced the Series 2 Genuine Collectible Pins. Those pins are now available through the Valve Store at welovefine.
Each package contains a code for a virtual version of the pin to display in-game. Also, these virtual pins are available as offers in-game and on the Steam Community Market.


HYPER BEAST

Looking to upgrade your equipment with a bold community-design? Check out the limited edition Hyper Beast Edition of the Steelseries Rival 300 Gaming Mouse and Mousepad!
